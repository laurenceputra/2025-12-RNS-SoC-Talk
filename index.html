What I Learned at SoC

Key skills and knowledge that benefited my career

CS is About Problem Solving

  • Your goal is not to learn to code
  • It's to learn how to solve problems from an engineering scientific perspective
  • Computer Science is about managing complexity
  • Break down a problem into smaller problems, and continue breaking it down until you have many small problems you can easily solve

Technical Skills

  • Programming fundamentals
  • Data structures and algorithms
  • Software engineering practices

Why Fundamentals Matter

  • Core fundamental skills vs usage level skills
  • Frameworks and tools change constantly, but fundamentals remain
  • Strong foundations help you adapt quickly to new technologies
  • Deep understanding lets you debug and optimize effectively

Soft Skills

  • Problem-solving mindset
  • Teamwork and collaboration
  • Communication and presentation

Tips & Advice for Freshmen

Academic Advice

  • Focus on fundamentals
  • Don't just chase grades
  • Take advantage of lab sessions

Focus on Delivering Value

  • What matters is the value you bring to the table
  • Don't chase certifications for the sake of collecting them
  • Instead, focus on skills that make a real impact

Be Like Water

  • Don't let a specific role become your identity
  • Be adaptable and open to new opportunities
  • Skills are transferable across roles and domains

Stay Curious & Keep Learning

  • Always keep learning - it can be anything
  • Curiosity drives innovation and growth
  • The tech landscape changes constantly - embrace it

Work-Life Balance

  • Work-life balance is good to have
  • But first, make sure your value is clear to others
  • Establish credibility before optimizing for balance

Financial Literacy

  • Understand basic personal finance early
  • Learn about budgeting, saving, and investing
  • Financial knowledge gives you freedom and options

Career Preparation

  • Start building your portfolio early
  • Network with seniors and industry professionals
  • Seek internship opportunities

Personal Development

  • Join CCAs and interest groups
  • Work on side projects
  • Stay curious and keep learning
